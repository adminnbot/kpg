Our Pool Services

Tailored solutions for Dubai's unique urban environment where dust, heat, and high usage demand specialized care.

  • ①
    Weekly Maintenance

    Comprehensive weekly cleaning program including skimming, vacuuming, brushing, and chemical balancing specifically formulated for Dubai's water and climate conditions. Our technicians are trained to handle the unique challenges of urban pool maintenance.

  • ②
    Filter Cleaning

    Specialized filter maintenance to combat Dubai's high dust levels. We service all filter types (sand, cartridge, DE) with thorough backwashing and media replacement when needed. Our approach prevents the clogging issues common in desert environments.

  • ③
    Chemical Balancing

    Precision water chemistry management to maintain perfect balance despite Dubai's extreme temperatures and heavy usage. We use premium chemicals that are safe for all pool surfaces and swimmers.

  • ④
    Equipment Repair

    Expert diagnosis and repair of pumps, heaters, automation systems, and other pool equipment. We work with all major brands and can source parts quickly in Dubai's market.

  • ⑤
    Tile & Surface Care

    Specialized cleaning to remove Dubai's hard water stains and scale buildup. We use non-abrasive techniques to restore your pool's finish without damage.
